Our Process

Solar PV Installations

New and existing solar systems installed, assessed and repaired. MCS compliant, SEG compatible, residential and commercial. With solar panels if you’re making more energy than your house needs, you are self sufficient and won’t draw any power from the grid. The excess produced energy is what charges up a solar battery. Once the sun goes down and the solar panels can’t provide power, the battery kicks in and continues to keep you independent until it drains.
